Our Specialty Services

Façade Remediation

Comprehensive building envelope restoration including sealant replacement, panel repairs, and weatherproofing systems for commercial and residential properties.

Parking Garage Restoration

Expert concrete repair, waterproofing, traffic coatings, and structural rehabilitation to extend parking facility lifespan and ensure safety compliance.

EIFS & Stucco Systems

Professional installation and repair of Exterior Insulation Finishing Systems with energy-efficient solutions and superior weather resistance.

Masonry Restoration

Complete brick, stone, and concrete masonry repair services including repointing, tuckpointing, and structural stabilization for heritage and modern buildings.

Waterproofing Systems

Advanced waterproofing solutions for foundations, roofs, and below-grade structures using proven materials and techniques to prevent water infiltration.

Metal Cladding

Custom metal panel installation, repair, and finishing for commercial facades including aluminum, steel, and composite cladding systems.
