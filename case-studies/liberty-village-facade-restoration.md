# Liberty Village Lofts Facade Restoration - Toronto

## Project Details
- **Client:** Liberty Village Lofts Condominium
- **Location:** Toronto, ON (Liberty Village)
- **Category:** Facade Restoration
- **Size:** 28,000 sq ft EIFS facade
- **Value:** $638,500
- **Duration:** 16 weeks
- **Your Role:** Prime Contractor

## The Challenge

A 12-story heritage condo conversion required complete EIFS facade restoration. The 1985 building showed extensive water damage from failed cladding systems.

### Primary Issues:
- 30% of EIFS delaminating from substrate
- Water infiltration causing $50K annual interior damage
- Heritage restrictions on facade modifications
- Occupied building (95% residents)

### Constraints:
- City of Toronto heritage approval required
- $650K board-approved budget
- Work restricted to weekdays 8am-5pm
- Match original brick aesthetic

### Client Goals:
1. Eliminate all water infiltration
2. Maintain heritage character
3. 15-year warranty coverage
4. Minimal resident disruption

## Our Solution

### Phase 1: Heritage Coordination (Week 1-3)
- Worked with City heritage planner
- Created custom color/texture mockups
- Obtained heritage permit approval
- Secured adjacent property access

### Phase 2: EIFS Removal & Substrate Repair (Week 4-8)
- Removed 8,400 sq ft failed EIFS sections
- Repaired deteriorated concrete substrate
- Installed new WRB and drainage plane
- Added insulation (R-20 thermal performance)

### Phase 3: New EIFS Application (Week 9-14)
- Applied Sto Gold Coat system (certified)
- Custom color matching (3 heritage-approved tones)
- Integrated window flashings
- Sealed all penetrations

### Phase 4: Final Details (Week 15-16)
- Caulking and sealant work
- Architectural trim restoration
- Final walkthrough with heritage officer
