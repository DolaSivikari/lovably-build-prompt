# Commerce Plaza Building Envelope Restoration - Toronto

## Project Details
- **Client:** Commerce Plaza Management Inc.
- **Location:** Downtown Toronto, ON
- **Category:** Building Envelope Restoration
- **Size:** 8-story commercial office
- **Value:** $1,240,000
- **Duration:** 22 weeks (weekends only)
- **Your Role:** Prime Contractor

## The Challenge

An occupied 8-story commercial office building required comprehensive envelope restoration including brick tuckpointing, concrete balcony repairs, and waterproofing - all while maintaining 100% office operations.

### Primary Issues:
- Deteriorating brick mortar (50+ years old)
- Spalling concrete balconies (24 units)
- Failed window perimeter sealants
- Water infiltration damaging tenant spaces

### Constraints:
- Work restricted to weekends only (no weekday disruption)
- 300+ office workers (Mon-Fri operations)
- Downtown location (strict noise bylaws)
- Weather delays (winter project)

### Client Goals:
1. Zero tenant disruption
2. Complete by fiscal year-end
3. 10-year warranty
4. Professional appearance maintained

## Our Solution

### Phase 1: Assessment & Planning (Week 1-2)
- Weekend-only condition survey
- Swing stage engineering approval
- Tenant communication plan
- Weekend work schedule (Sat 7am - Sun 7pm)

### Phase 2: Masonry Restoration (Week 3-12)
- Tuckpointing: 18,500 linear feet
- Brick replacement: 2,400 units
- Limestone lintel repairs
- Completed in 10-hour weekend shifts

### Phase 3: Concrete Repairs (Week 13-18)
- 24 balcony restorations
- Rebar replacement and epoxy coating
- Waterproofing membrane installation
- Traffic coatings applied

### Phase 4: Sealant & Waterproofing (Week 19-22)
- 3,200 linear feet window perimeter sealant
- Expansion joint replacement
- Parapet coping waterproofing
- Final inspections

### Weekend-Only Approach:
- Friday 6pm: Swing stage setup
- Saturday 7am - Sunday 7pm: Work
- Sunday 8pm: Site cleanup, stage removal
- Monday 7am: Building ready for business
